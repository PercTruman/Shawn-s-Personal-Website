My friend Louis and I are also disc golf fanatics, and we've been playing this sport for about 10 years as well. The game uses all of the same terminology as standard golf, but you use golf discs (not to be confused with frisbees) rather than clubs and balls. Typically, courses are set up in nature parks, but since the sport is really beginning to explode, sometimes disc golf can be played on regular golf courses as well. The targets are baskets equipped with chains that catch the frisbee as it lands in the basket.
